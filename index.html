Pittsburgh Steelers

The Pittsburgh Steelers are a professional American football team based in Pittsburgh. The Steelers compete in the National Football League as a member of the American Football Conference North division. Founded in 1933, the Steelers are the seventh-oldest franchise in the NFL, and the oldest franchise in the AFC.

  1. History of the Steelers
    • 6-time Super Bowl Champions
    • 21 division championships
    • Coaching Icons: Chuck Noll, Bill Cowher, and Mike Tomlin, Mike McCarthy
    • 32 Hall of Fame players or coaches
    • Reputation for hard-nosed football: "Men of Steel"
  2. Legends of the Team
    • Terry Bradshaw (4-time Super Bowl champion)
    • Ben Roethlisberger (2-time Super Bowl champion)
    • T.J. Watt (2021 Defensive Player of the Year)
    • Troy Polamalu (Best safety to ever play the game)
